  Welcome to the Switcheo Explorer!

  The purpose of this software is to allow convenient querying of a user's balance, transfers, orders and trading volume across NEO & ETHEREUM networks. You just need input your addresses and click , and you will see balances all your  tokens, wich are stored in contracts of Switcheo exchange, all your orders, all your transfers and all your volume for each tokens.

Features:

1. Smart contract's balance. 
2. Smart contract's transfers.
3. Orders history.
4. Trading volume for each token.
5. Total exchange balance in dollars for all Switcheo's contracts.
6. Volume fees of tokens.
7. Status monitor of switcheo's nodes in real time.
8. NEO network in Interactive 3D graph.
9. Export data to excel and csv format.

How to try out? 

1. Follow this links to: http://switcheoexplorer.tech or http://switchstatistic.000webhostapp.com
2. Choose the desired contract or leave the default values.
3. Input your NEO or ETH address(es).
4. Press the green button or 'Enter'.
